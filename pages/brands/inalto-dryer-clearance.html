Inalto Dryer Clearance Requirements

Inalto dryer installs look simple until the wall gap, venting plan and door access are counted properly. This page gives you the clearance numbers first so the shortlist stays realistic. Current guide figures are 20mm at each side, 50mm at the rear and 50mm above. The side gap is 10mm tighter than the usual category baseline. The rear allowance is 30mm tighter than the default figure. The top allowance is 150mm tighter than the default figure.

Installation Tips

  • Check the wall gap and lint service access before the dryer is pushed fully home.
  • If the dryer is stacked, measure from the finished floor to the underside of the shelf or cupboard above.
  • Confirm the venting plan before delivery if the model is not condenser or heat-pump.
