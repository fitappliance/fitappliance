Franke Dishwasher Clearance Requirements

Franke dishwasher fit checks usually come down to side room, service depth and how tidy the cavity opening really is. Start with these figures, then move to the model list. Current guide figures are 20mm at each side, 50mm at the rear and 50mm above. The side gap is 18mm wider than the usual category baseline. The top allowance is 25mm taller than the default figure.

Installation Tips

  • Measure the cavity after the floor finish and kickboard are installed, not before.
  • Check the service void and hose route at the rear before you commit to the cabinet depth.
  • Level the dishwasher with the door open once the side gaps are confirmed.
