Hisense Fridge Clearance Requirements

Hisense fridges cover a broad spread of widths, which makes early filtering worthwhile. Check the clearances here first, then compare the models that still fit the opening you have. Current guide figures are 20mm at each side, 50mm at the rear and 50mm above. The side gap is 20mm tighter than the usual category baseline. The rear allowance is 25mm deeper than the default figure.

Installation Tips

  • Check the wall, benchtop and overhead cabinet as separate measurements before delivery day.
  • Leave enough room to level the cabinet after it is in position, not just enough room to slide it in.
  • Keep the installation manual beside the tape measure so the airflow note is checked against the real cavity.
