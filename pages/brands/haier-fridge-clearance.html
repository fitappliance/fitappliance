Haier Fridges Clearance Requirements

Haier fridges installed in Australian homes require 25.4mm side, 25.4mm rear, and 25.4mm top ventilation clearance per manufacturer guidelines. Notable: narrower side clearance than most brands (25.4mm vs 40mm average), which can help in tighter cavities; unusually low top clearance requirement (25.4mm vs 50mm default) — good for fitted kitchen cabinetry. Insufficient clearance can void warranty and cause premature compressor failure.
