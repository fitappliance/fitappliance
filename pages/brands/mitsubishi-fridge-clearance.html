Mitsubishi Fridge Clearance Requirements

Mitsubishi fridge installs usually get tight at the top panel and rear wall. This page brings the working clearance numbers together and points you to the 1 models we track for this range. Current guide figures are 30mm at each side, 30mm at the rear and 50mm above. The side gap is 10mm tighter than the usual category baseline. The rear allowance is 5mm deeper than the default figure.

Installation Tips

  • Check the wall, benchtop and overhead cabinet as separate measurements before delivery day.
  • Leave enough room to level the cabinet after it is in position, not just enough room to slide it in.
  • Keep the installation manual beside the tape measure so the airflow note is checked against the real cavity.
