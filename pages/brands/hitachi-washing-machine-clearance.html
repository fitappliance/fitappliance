Installation Tips

  • Push washing machine at least 50mm from the wall before securing — use a tape measure, not eyeballing.
  • If installing under overhead cabinetry, confirm 50mm gap above the lid or top panel.
  • Measure your cavity before purchase — washing machine dimensions vary by ±20mm between models within this brand.
