📋 FitAppliance.com.au is committed to protecting your privacy in accordance with the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Spam Act 2003.

1. What information we collect

Information you provide

  • Appliance dimensions: Width, height, depth measurements you enter into the search tool. This data is processed client-side in your browser and is not transmitted to our servers unless you use a save/share feature.
  • Postcode: Not required for the core fit checker. If a future local feature asks for it, it should be processed client-side unless that feature explicitly says otherwise.
  • Email address: Only collected if you opt in to receive saved search results or newsletter communications. See Section 5.

Information collected automatically

  • Log data: IP address, browser type, pages visited, referral URL, time and date of visits. Standard server logs retained for 90 days.
  • Performance diagnostics: Aggregated Core Web Vitals telemetry (LCP, INP, CLS, TTFB) sampled at 10% and processed without user identifiers.

2. How we use your information

We use collected information solely to:

  • Operate and improve the appliance search tool
  • Understand aggregate usage patterns (e.g. which appliance categories are most searched)
  • Send you communications you have explicitly opted into
  • Comply with legal obligations

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Cookies and tracking

FitAppliance uses the following cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Session management and security. Cannot be disabled without breaking site functionality.
  • Advertising cookies: Google AdSense may use cookies to serve relevant ads.
  • Affiliate cookies: Set by retailer affiliate networks (Commission Factory, Partnerize) when you click a retailer link. These are third-party cookies used to track commission attribution. See Section 5.

You can manage cookies via your browser settings. Blocking all cookies may impact site functionality.

4. Real user performance monitoring (RUM)

FitAppliance runs lightweight Real User Monitoring to measure page performance in production.

  • Sample rate: 10% of page views (`Math.random() < 0.1`)
  • Metrics: LCP, INP, CLS, and TTFB
  • Transport: `navigator.sendBeacon` to same-origin endpoint `/api/rum`
  • Stored fields: metric name, metric value, path (without query), timestamp, and truncated user agent
  • Not collected: IP addresses, cookies, user IDs, form inputs, or referer query parameters

5. Affiliate tracking

When you click a retailer link on FitAppliance, the affiliate network (Commission Factory or Partnerize) sets a tracking cookie on your device. This cookie:

  • Records that you arrived at the retailer via FitAppliance
  • Persists for the cookie window duration (14–30 days depending on retailer)
  • Enables us to receive a commission if you complete a purchase
  • Does not give us access to your payment details or retailer account

Affiliate tracking is governed by each network's privacy policy: Commission Factory Privacy Policy and Partnerize Privacy Policy.

6. Email communications — Spam Act 2003

FitAppliance complies strictly with the Australian Spam Act 2003. We will only send you commercial email if:

  • You have given us express, opt-in consent (e.g. by checking a clearly labelled checkbox — never pre-checked)
  • Each email clearly identifies FitAppliance as the sender
  • Each email contains a functional unsubscribe mechanism that processes your request within 5 business days

Subscription delivery is handled by Buttondown. When you submit the newsletter form, your email is sent directly to Buttondown for list management and confirmation email delivery. FitAppliance does not store subscriber email addresses in the repository, analytics payloads, or browser performance logs.

Each message contains a one-click unsubscribe link managed by Buttondown. You can opt out at any time.

We do not send unsolicited commercial electronic messages. We do not share your email address with third parties for marketing purposes.

7. Third-party services

  • Google AdSense — Advertising. Google may use cookies to serve relevant ads. Google Ad Policy
  • Vercel — Hosting provider and function runtime. RUM events are processed via same-origin serverless function and standard platform logs. Vercel Privacy Policy
  • Buttondown — Email subscription management and unsubscribe processing. Buttondown Privacy Policy
  • Commission Factory / Partnerize — Affiliate tracking. See Section 5.

FitAppliance does not control third-party services' privacy practices. We recommend reviewing their privacy policies.

8. Your rights under Australian Privacy Law

Under the Australian Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles (APPs), you have the right to:

  • Access personal information we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Complain to the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) if you believe your privacy rights have been violated

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@fitappliance.com.au. For OAIC complaints: oaic.gov.au.
