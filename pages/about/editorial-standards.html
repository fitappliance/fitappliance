Editorial Standards

Last updated: 18 April 2026

FitAppliance content is written and generated under strict data-integrity constraints for Australian appliance fit guidance.

1. Accuracy and Source Policy

  • We publish only values sourced from project datasets or documented official references.
  • Unknown values remain explicitly unknown; they are never estimated for presentation.
  • Schema fields without source support are omitted by design.

2. Commercial Independence

  • Affiliate links are disclosed at the point of interaction.
  • Ranking and fit logic are not altered by affiliate commission rates.
  • No sponsored placement is shown as an organic fit result.

3. Structured Data Governance

  • JSON-LD blocks are validated in automated tests.
  • `aggregateRating` and `reviewCount` are prohibited unless real source data is available.
  • Product and organization schema are generated from available factual fields only.

4. Update and Correction Workflow

  • Regenerated content is tested before deployment.
  • If a data issue is identified, the corresponding source is corrected and pages are regenerated.
  • Material methodology changes are documented in `/methodology`.
