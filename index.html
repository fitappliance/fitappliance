ACCC & ACL Compliance

All affiliate relationships are disclosed immediately adjacent to purchase links — not buried in footers. Rankings are based on objective criteria (dimensional fit, energy rating, price) and are never influenced by commission rates. Brand exclusions (e.g. Miele, Asko) are clearly noted.

NCC 2022 Dryer Notice

Under the National Construction Code 2022, vented tumble dryers can no longer exhaust into roof spaces in new builds. If you select "Apartment" as your dwelling type, we automatically filter to condenser and heat-pump dryer models only.
